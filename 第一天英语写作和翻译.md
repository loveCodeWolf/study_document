# 浙江专升本英语每日练习（写作+翻译）

## 一、英语写作范文（高频话题：大学生活/校园活动）

### 范文原文

How to Make the Most of College Life

As college students, we are entering a crucial stage of our lives that is full of opportunities and challenges. It is of great significance for us to make rational use of our college time and strive to become better versions of ourselves.

First and foremost, we should attach great importance to our academic performance. Attending classes on time, taking detailed notes and reviewing what we have learned regularly are effective ways to master professional knowledge. In addition, we ought to participate in various campus activities actively, which can not only enrich our spare time but also help us develop practical skills and make more friends.

Last but not least, we should cultivate a positive attitude towards life. When facing difficulties and setbacks, we should keep calm and seek solutions actively instead of giving up easily. Only in this way can we gain valuable experience and grow into responsible and capable adults.

In conclusion, college life is a precious gift for us. We should cherish every moment, make full use of the resources around us and work hard to pursue our dreams. I believe that with our efforts, we will have a meaningful and wonderful college life.

### 范文详细解析（通用句子+固定搭配+用法说明）

#### （一）通用句子及用法（可直接套用，适配多数写作话题）

- 1. As + 身份/名词, we are entering a crucial stage of our lives that is full of opportunities and challenges.（开头引入话题，适配“大学生活、成长、学习、环保”等话题）
       
用法：As引导原因状语从句（表“作为……”），that引导定语从句修饰stage；crucial（重要的）替换common，提升句式高级感；full of opportunities and challenges（充满机遇和挑战）是万能搭配，用于开头引出话题的重要性。
      

- 2. It is of great significance for us to do sth.（强调做某事的重要性，万能开头/中间过渡句）
        
用法：It是形式主语，真正主语是后面的to do sth.；of great significance = very significant（非常重要的），比直接用very significant更符合专升本写作的正式语气；替换句式：It is very important for us to do sth.（基础版）。
      

- 3. First and foremost, ... In addition, ... Last but not least, ...（分点论述，逻辑清晰，适配所有议论文、说明文）
        
用法：First and foremost（首先，最重要的是）= First（基础版）；In addition（此外）= Besides/What’s more（同义替换，避免重复）；Last but not least（最后但同样重要的是）= Finally（基础版），三个短语连用，让文章结构更严谨，专升本写作中使用可加分。
      

- 4. Only in this way can we ...（倒装句，强调方式方法，提升句式高级感）
        
用法：Only+状语（in this way/by doing sth.等）置于句首，句子需用部分倒装（情态动词can提前）；适配结尾总结、提出建议，比如“Only by working hard can we achieve our goals.”（只有通过努力，我们才能实现目标）。

- 5. In conclusion, ...（结尾总结，万能收尾）
        
用法：In conclusion = In a word/To sum up（同义替换），后面接总结性观点，呼应开头，让文章首尾呼应；结尾可搭配“I believe that...”（我相信……），增强情感色彩和说服力。
      

#### （二）固定搭配（高频考点，必须掌握）

- 1. make rational use of 合理利用（替换make good use of，更贴合正式写作）
        
例句：We should make rational use of our spare time to improve ourselves.
      

- 2. strive to do sth. 努力做某事（替换try to do sth.，高级替换）
        
例句：All students should strive to achieve their academic goals.
      

- 3. attach great importance to sth. 重视某事（专升本高频搭配，必考）
        
用法：attach importance to + 名词/动名词，比如attach great importance to learning English（重视学英语）。
      

- 4. take detailed notes 做详细笔记（贴合学生话题，写作/翻译均可能考）

- 5. review what we have learned 复习我们所学的内容（宾语从句搭配，提升句式复杂度）

- 6. participate in 参加（替换take part in，正式语气，适配校园活动、社会活动话题）

- 7. enrich one’s spare time 丰富某人的业余生活（校园、生活类话题万能搭配）

- 8. develop practical skills 培养实践能力（大学生活、职业规划话题高频搭配）

- 9. face difficulties and setbacks 面对困难和挫折（成长、励志类话题必备）

- 10. seek solutions actively 积极寻找解决办法（提出建议类句式搭配）

#### （三）易错点提醒

- 1. 主谓一致：As college students, we are...（主语we，谓语用复数are，避免误写为is）；

- 2. 定语从句引导词：stage that is full of...（先行词stage是物，引导词用that/which，不可省略）；

- 3. 倒装句结构：Only in this way can we...（can必须提前，不可写成Only in this way we can...）；

- 4. 固定搭配介词：participate **in**（不可用on/at）；attach importance **to**（to是介词，后面接动名词，不可接动词原形）。
